PANORMO VILLAGE

Panormo is a traditional coastal village about twenty kilometers east of the city of Rethymno. It's easily accessed by car or bus and the nearest airport is in Heraklion, in a distance of 55 kilometers. It's a quite tourist resort, which is highly developed in the past few years without loosing its traditional and picturesque character.

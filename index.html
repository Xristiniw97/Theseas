WELCOME TO THEASEA RESIDENCES

IN PANORMO, RETHYMNO

Welcome to THEASEA STYLISH RESIDENCES, a beautiful complex of apartments which is designed to offer unforgettable moments of calm and relaxation. All the apartments combine the bright glow of the sun and the colors of the Cretan land and sea, with a panoramic view to a leafy olive grove.

PANORMO VILLAGE

Panormo is a traditional coastal village about twenty kilometers east of the city of Rethymno. It's easily accessed by car or bus and the nearest airport is in Heraklion, in a distance of 55 kilometers. It's a quite tourist resort, which is highly developed in the past few years without loosing its traditional and picturesque character.
